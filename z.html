★ Phishing Scams

Email Phishing: Scammers send emails that appear to be from legitimate sources, such as banks or government agencies, to trick individuals into providing personal information.

Website Phishing: Fraudulent websites imitate real websites to trick users into entering sensitive information like usernames and passwords.

Example: A phishing email that appears to be from a bank, requesting the recipient to click on a link and provide their login credentials.
