★ Social Media Scams

Impersonation: Scammers create fake profiles on social media platforms to impersonate friends, family, or public figures and request money or personal information.

Clickbait Scams: Fraudulent links lead users to phishing sites or sites that deliver malware.

Example: Posing as a colleague and requesting sensitive information via email, claiming it's required urgently.
