★ Why Internet Safety Is Important ?

  • Reduces the chances of any unauthorized access to personal data or property.
  • Provides an opportunity for individuals to connect and share information safely.
  • Helps to secure online transactions and while using digital wallets on mobiles.
  • Protects against unauthorized network access or hacking.
  • Protects against phishing, email frauds, and spamming.
  • Protects against cyberterrorism, which is a threat to national security.
  • Protects against risks and harms resulting in stolen information, online scams like identity theft, unsafe communication, and even mental and physical harm.
