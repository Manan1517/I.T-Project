★ Tech Support Scams

Fake Tech Support Calls: Scammers call, claiming to be from a reputable tech company, and inform users of a non-existent problem with their computer. They then offer to fix it for a fee or gain remote access to the computer.

Cold calls from fake Microsoft technicians stating that the victim's computer has a virus and needs immediate attention.
