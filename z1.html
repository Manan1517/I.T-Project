★ Online Shopping Scams

Fake Online Stores: Scammers set up fake online stores to sell non-existent products or counterfeit goods.

Overpayment Scams: Scammers overpay for an item with a fraudulent check and ask the seller to refund the excess amount.

Example: Setting up a fake online store offering high-demand products at unrealistically low prices.
